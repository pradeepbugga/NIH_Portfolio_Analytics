The NIH Audit
CATEGORY DEFINITIONS
Mechanistic / Basic Science Studies fundamental biology and disease mechanisms.
Therapeutic Tests or develops treatments that act on disease biology.
Diagnostic Develops or validates tests to detect or predict disease.
Research Tool Builds broadly reusable methods, assays, or platforms.
Clinical / Health Systems Improves how care is delivered, accessed, or used.
Observational Epidemiology Studies population patterns and risk factors without intervention.
Research Infrastructure Supports shared resources, networks, or research programs.
Education / Training Trains researchers or clinicians and builds workforce capacity.
